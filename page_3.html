Investigation

Entry 1

The Riddler struck again tonight, claiming the life of Mayor Mitchell, a bitter reminder of Gotham's festering underbelly. His cryptic clues and macabre modus operandi are a labyrinthine puzzle that I am committed to solving. The city is on edge, and it falls on me to restore order.

Entry 2

Commissioner Savage has become the Riddler's next pawn in his deadly game, another life extinguished too soon. Each riddle left behind is a piece of a greater enigma, a chilling breadcrumb trail that I must decipher before more innocent lives are lost.

Entry 3

A significant lead tonight: a thumb drive hidden in Mitchell's car, containing images linking him to a woman, Annika Kosolov, last seen at the Iceberg Lounge. The club's manager, Cobblepot, or as he prefers, "The Penguin" is a notorious figure in Gotham's underworld. The threads of corruption seem to weave deeper into the city's fabric.

Entry 4

The Riddler’s audacity knows no bounds. He orchestrated a sick display at Mitchell's funeral, turning DA Colson into a live bomb. A triple riddle challenge, a deadly ultimatum, and Colson paid the price. The mention of an informant within Falcone's operation complicates matters further. Suspicions lead back to The Penguin.

Entry 5

An operation tonight turned up a grim discovery - Annika Kosolov's lifeless body. It's clear now - The Riddler isn't just punishing the corrupt, he's unraveling Gotham's entire power structure. His obsession with my family is disturbing, his anger tied to a twisted vendetta against the Waynes. Alfred's been hospitalized, another victim of the Riddler's relentless game.

Entry 6

A shocking revelation - Selina Kyle is Falcone's daughter. And Falcone was the informant. Before we could bring him in, a sniper's bullet ended his life, courtesy of the Riddler. The man behind the mask is Edward Nashton, a forensics accountant. His deadly vendetta, however, is far from over.
